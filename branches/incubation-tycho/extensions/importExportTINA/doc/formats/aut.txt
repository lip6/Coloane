
Format .aut:
------------

It is  the lts description  format of the  Aldebaran tool of  the CADP
toolset (see the CADP documents).

NOTE: the  .aut format does not  allow double quotes (")  to appear in
labels.   In addition  tina  forbids backquotes  (`)  in .aut  labels.
Backquotes are used by tina  to encode lists of properties into single
.aut labels.
